Common questions

How do I invite a co-parent?

Open Settings, then use Send Invite Link in the Family section to share the calendar with the other parent.

Where do holiday assignments live?

Holidays are managed in the Holidays tab, where each year can be assigned to a parent and synced across the shared family calendar.

Can I see what changed?

Yes. Zoey keeps assignment history so each parent can review who changed dates, when the update happened, and which days were affected.

How do I contact support?

Use kohli.varun5@gmail.com for product, sync, or privacy questions.

Privacy policy

Simple and product-specific

What Zoey stores

Zoey stores calendar settings, day assignments, holiday definitions, holiday assignments, and assignment history needed to run the shared family calendar.

What stays on your device

The child's profile photo is stored locally on each device. It is not synced to the other parent through Zoey.

How sync works

Shared family data syncs through Apple's iCloud using CloudKit. The app does not run its own separate account system for this sync.

Tracking and ads

Zoey does not declare third-party tracking and does not declare collected data types in its current Apple privacy manifest.

Full policy

Zoey Calendar is designed to help co-parents coordinate schedules, holiday assignments, and change history. To provide those features, the app stores shared calendar data in the user's iCloud environment through CloudKit. This includes family settings, parent names and colors, date assignments, holiday records, and assignment history entries.

Child photos are handled differently. The app stores the child's photo locally on each device and does not sync that image through iCloud to the other parent. This keeps photo handling separate from the shared schedule data.

Zoey's current privacy manifest declares no tracking and no collected data types. It declares limited access to file timestamps and UserDefaults only for app functionality. The app may request notification permission so users can receive calendar change alerts.

Zoey does not sell personal information. If the product's data handling changes materially, this page should be updated before or when the change ships.

Privacy questions can be sent to kohli.varun5@gmail.com.
